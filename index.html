    How to buy $xAI

    01

    1. Buying and Transferring ETH

    As mentioned earlier, to buy xAI, you need to purchase ETH. You can purchase ETH on Coinbase or Binance or any other exchanges. You can even buy ETH directly inside MetaMask. If you have already bought ETH, you can focus on the Transfer part discussed below. Once you bought ETH, you need to transfer that into MetaMask. Send ETH from Coinbase to MetaMask or Send ETH from Binance to MetaMask.

    2. Connect your MetaMask wallet

    Go to the Uniswap website. Once you are on the Uniswap website, your MetaMask wallet will connect with it automatically. If it didn’t, you can manually connect it by clicking the Connect button, which is inside the MetaMask wallet or on the Uniswap website.

    02

    03

    3. Import xAI Contract

    After establishing the connection between Uniswap and MetaMask, you need to import the xAI contract. This is where you need to be very careful. Ensure to copy and paste the correct xAI contract, or else you could buy the wrong token. xAI Contract Address: Xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx To paste the xAI Contract address on the Uniswap website, Click on Select a token Click on Import (Upon clicking Import, paste the xAI contract address)

    4. Enter the amount

    You can enter the amount of ETH that you want to swap, or you can enter the amount of xAI you want. Make sure to leave some ETH for the gas fee. Otherwise, your transaction will fail.

    04

    05

    5. Tune-up Uniswap setting

    Immediately after importing the xAI contract, Uniswap will fetch the best price for the swap. In case you have been prompted with a “No Liquidity” message, Click on Trade Using V2 Click on the wheel icon top right corner of the Swap window Set Slippage Tolerance between 4-5%

    6. Swap ETH for xAI

    Now, you can see Uniswap fetching the best swap price for you. Review the gas fee and then click Swap.

    06

    07

    7. Confirm Swap

    Immediately after clicking the Swap, you can see the confirmation pop-up. Review your transaction details and click Confirm Swap. Note: Since the price gets updated every few seconds, you need to be fast while reviewing the details. And always keep an eye on the gas fee; if the total order amount (along with the gas fee) exceeds your total ETH balance, your transaction will fail.

    8. Add xAI to MetaMask wallet

    Click on ‘Import tokens’, located at the bottom of the ‘Assets’ tab on your wallet homepage. Switch to the ‘Custom token’ tab at the top, and then enter the xAI token address in the first field. If you still can’t find the token details, try locating the token’s documentation through a web search. When you click ‘Import Tokens’, the token type will be added to your wallet.

    09

    9. Check your MetaMask wallet

    Once you see the transaction notification successful, you need to go to your MetaMask wallet extension and check your wallet balance. That’s it. Congratulations on buying xAI. Note: We are not financial advisors and this is not an investment recommendation.
